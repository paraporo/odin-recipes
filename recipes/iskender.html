Iskender Recipe

Description

Iskender is a turkish dish dating back to the Ottoman empire.

It consists of slices of meat on top of sliced bread with tomato sauce on top.

It is commonly served with yogurt on the side

Ingredients

  • Slices of meat
  • Sliced bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Bake bread
  2. Prepare tomato sauce
  3. Slice the spining meat
  4. Cut the bread into small pieces
  5. Put together plate with bread at the bottom, meat on the top, drizzls of tomato sauce and yogurt on the side
  6. Enjoy!
